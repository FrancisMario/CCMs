Why Use CCMS

Managing a club is not an easy task. Keeping records and managing members is not an easy going on the side of the executives, especially if the club has too many members.
Camcoders Club Management System CCMS is here to wipe off the nightmares of managing a club. With the CCMS you can keep records of each member of the club from registration to certification, monitor attendance, and monitor finacial activities of the club at a glance.

Financial

With the CCMS you can assess your financial position, formulate plans to increase income and cut on cost of running the club.

Time Saving

With the CCMS you can conduct operations faster, better and more efficiently. Thus saves you time so you can worry about other things.
